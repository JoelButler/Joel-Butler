Garibaldi Lake

I originally started hiking with the intention of summiting Black Tusk, or Panorama Ridge. However, when I reached the trail junction at the 10km mark, the tusk and ridge were both hidden by clouds. In addition to this, the trail changed from bare to slushy. I decided I didn't want to add an additional 10km to my hike in variable conditions and head down to Garibaldi Lake.

I was very happy to see that the lake wasn't also covered by clouds! Last time I was here, it was frozen over and covered in snow. The colour of the lake can't be appreciated in pictures. It is as usual, better in person.

A plan on bringing a delicious homemade sandwich for each summit I attempt to climb going forward. Alpine Sandwiches is a website that showcases submitted summit sandwiches! Hopefully I can make it on here eventually. The Garibaldi Lake sandwich was inspired by a delicious sushi roll and is made with smoked salmon, mango, cucumber, and cream cheese on a ciabatta bun!
