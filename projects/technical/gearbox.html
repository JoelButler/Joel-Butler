Gearbox Design

This university project produced amazing results! I won't get too deep into any technical details as i could go on for hours.

During mechanical design, student teams were required to design a complete mid-drive gearbox for an electric powered bicycle. This project included extensive design work. The final report included fully detailed drawings for all shafts, in addition to a standard cutaway drawings showing the assembly of the gearbox. The report also included all the cumulative design details worked on throughout the project to form a complete engineering report.

Detailed design for gears utilized the AGMA methods for analysis of bending and surface fatigue. Furthermore, all critical failure locations on shafts for fatigue failure are analyzed using the stress-life method in conjunction with Minerâ€™s damage rule. Detailed design drawings for shafts were generated including details of shoulders, chamfers, keyways, diameters, grooves, threads, etc. Bearings are selected and analyzed using the SKF on-line catalogue.
