JOEL BUTLER

Drawing


I don’t spend too much time drawing, but it’s a cool skill none the less. My sister is a professional tattoo artist and growing up alongside her, I was exposed to a ton of drawings, painting, and art in general. I don’t practice drawing but I happen to be quite decent at it. I believe this skill comes from having a strong visual understanding of objects and being able to replicate sizes and shapes precisely. Maybe I learned from watching her? I actually don’t really know anything about drawing technique. Either way, this skill really helps me when drawing in AutoCAD, Solidworks, hand sketching ideas, and Pictionary.
