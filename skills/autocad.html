As part of a projectâ€™s installation design, a snatch block was chosen to raise a device weighing up to 16 tons. The existing 20 ton snatch block makes use of a shackle to connect to other objects. For this project, a new lifting apparatus was required in place of the shackle. A simple design made of readily available and easy to manufacture material was required. The design links the 16 ton body to the snatch block by means of two pins and two plates. Based on the required size of the shackle pin, it is clear that this is the weakest point in the design. Therefore, engineering calculations of shear stress, bearing stress, and normal stress, were carried out at this location to determine if the design will meet the required factor of safety.After confirming engineering calculations, the design was sent to a professional structural engineer for final approval.
